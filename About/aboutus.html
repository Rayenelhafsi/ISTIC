About Us

Our SIGHT Group, a young IEEE student branch at ISTIC (founded Dec. 2023), leverages technology for community impact. We champion innovation, tackle local challenges (water security, education, social support), and promote ethical tech use. Through projects like the Warka Project (clean water collection with IoT), Istic platform and successful conferences, we empower our community.

Overview

This project aims to create a digital platform for Higher Institute of Information and Communication Technologies students. The platform will provide access to course information, exam materials, and a grade calculation tool. By enhancing student resources and academic management, this project contributes to the UN's Sustainable Development Goal of Quality Education (SDG 4).
